
## Scenerio 
A horrible wildfire has broken out, and you need to decide the people's conditions and see who should be treated first inside a hospital.

## Wildfire-triage rules 
Patients are in a priority queue/tier; severe people are treated first, while expectants are last. 

Severe: in very bad condition, but can be saved 

delayed: greatly injured but can wait to be treated 

Minimal: few to no injuries and can be treated later 


expectant: people who are too injured and cannot be saved because of time constraints or no chance of being saved
