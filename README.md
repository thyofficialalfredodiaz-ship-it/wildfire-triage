
## Senerio 
A horrible wild fire outbrak has happend and you need to decide the people conditions and see who should be treated first inside a hospital.

## wildfire-triage rules 
paitents are in a priority queue/tier, severe people are treated first while expectants are last. 

Severe: in very bad condition but can be saved 
delayed: greatly injured but can wait to be treated 
minimal: few to no injuries and can be treated later 
expectant: people who are too injured and cant be saved because of time contraints or no chance of being saved
